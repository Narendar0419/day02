Welcome to 717 Car Garage

about us

At 717 Car Garage, we believe that every vehicle deserves expert care and every customer deserves honest, dependable service.
With more than 25 years of experience in the automotive industry,
we have established ourselves as a trusted destination for vehicle maintenance, diagnostics, and repair solutions.
Since our founding in 2001, our goal has remained the same: to provide high-quality automotive services at fair prices while building lasting relationships with our customers.
What started as a small neighborhood workshop has grown into a respected automotive service center known for professionalism, technical expertise, and customer satisfaction

Our Journey

717 Car Garage was founded by a team of passionate automotive professionals who recognized the need for a repair center
that combined technical excellence with transparency and integrity. Over the years, we have continually expanded our capabilities,
invested in advanced diagnostic technology, and trained our technicians to stay up to date with the latest developments in the automotive industry.
From routine oil changes to complex engine repairs, we have successfully serviced thousands of vehicles and earned the trust of individual car owners, families, and businesses alike.



Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver reliable, efficient,
and affordable automotive solutions while maintaining the highest standards of workmanship and customer care.
We strive to ensure that every vehicle leaving our garage performs safely, efficiently, and reliably.
